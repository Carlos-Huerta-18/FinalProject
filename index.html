General Information

  • Health Issues Caused By Pollution

    Spare The Air


    High air pollution levels can cause immediate health problems including:

    • Aggravated cardiovascular and respiratory illness
    • Added stress to heart and lungs, which must work harder to supply the body with oxygen
    • Damaged cells in the respiratory system

  • Enviornmental Effects

    livescience

    • Land can become polluted by household garbage and by industrial waste
    • Water pollution happens when chemicals or dangerous foreign substances are introduced to water, including chemicals, sewage, pesticides and fertilizers from agricultural runoff, or metals like lead or mercury
    • Air pollution happens when people release particles into the air from burning fuels

  • The Chemistry Of Oil

    Oil:
    A viscous liquid derived from petroleum, especially for use as a fuel or lubricant.

    • Oil doesn't mix with water
    • The substance oil is extremely flamable
    • Oil is made through the process of cracking

    Cracking:
    The process whereby complex organic molecules such as kerogens or long-chain hydrocarbons are broken down into simpler molecules such as light hydrocarbons.

  • Hydrocarbons

    Hydrocarbons: An organic compound consisting entirely of hydrogen and carbon.
    there are three types of hydrocarbons:alkanes, alkenes, alkynes.
    Alkane:any of the series of saturated hydrocarbons including methane, ethane, propane, and higher members.

    • Alkanes have single bonds

    Alkene:any of the series of unsaturated hydrocarbons containing a double bond, including ethylene and propylene.
    Alkyne:any of the series of unsaturated hydrocarbons containing a triple bond, including acetylene.
